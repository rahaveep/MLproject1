Instructions on sending files via scp: https://linuxize.com/post/how-to-use-scp-command-to-securely-transfer-files/

Send from laptop to workstation:

- navigate to directory your file is saved in
- type in terminal from laptop: 
 $ scp filename1 niccolo@10.208.28.17:filepath
- the file will be saved on the filepath declared on the workstation
